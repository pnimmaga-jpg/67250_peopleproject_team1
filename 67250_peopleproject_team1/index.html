TartanResearch connects CMU faculty and students so that the research participation process becomes easier. Faculty share projects, and students discover and apply to research opportunities.

01 — Problem & Context

Research shouldn't be this hard to find.

* Students struggle to find research roles due to outdated info, cold emailing, and unclear starting points.

* At Carnegie Mellon, opportunities exist, but no structured system connects students with faculty.

* Research access is unequal: well-connected students find opportunities, while others are overlooked; faculty struggle to reach the right candidates.

* TartanResearch solves this with a centralized, transparent platform connecting CMU students and faculty directly.

02 — Strategic Analysis

Value creation through structured connection.

* Students gain clear access to verified opportunities, eliminating information gaps and guesswork.

* Faculty receive structured, relevant applications instead of scattered, unqualified outreach.

* Unlike broad platforms, TartanResearch is purpose-built for academic research workflows and timelines.

* A streamlined pipeline connects posting, discovery, and selection into a transparent system.

03 — Social Theories

Adoption, acceptance, and equity.

Technology Acceptance Model

* Easy-to-use design and clear benefits drive adoption for both students and faculty.

* Verified access and trust signals reinforce system credibility and usability.

Equity & Inclusion Frameworks

* The platform removes reliance on connections by making all opportunities visible and accessible.

* It shifts research access from privilege-based to merit-based participation.

Diffusion of Innovations

* Network effects increase value as more faculty post and more students apply.

* Institutional adoption enables rapid spread within CMU and potential expansion to other universities.

04 — Expected Impact

What changes — and for whom.

* Reduces recruitment friction and improves match quality between students and labs.

* Provides institutions with clearer visibility into research participation.

* Expands equitable access to research, impacting academic and career trajectories.

* Scales a model that could address similar access gaps across universities.

05 — Team & Process

Built collaboratively, contributed to distinctly.

* Each team member led a core area: problem analysis, system design, development, and UX.

* Final deliverables were completed collaboratively, ensuring consistency across all components.

* A clear team structure enabled fast progress without isolating contributions.

* Coordination and shared ownership kept the project cohesive from start to finish.
